# Determining Liability in AI-related Medical Errors

Research project examining how existing legal and ethical frameworks handle **AI-driven medical errors**, and what safeguards are needed as AI adoption expands in healthcare.  [oai_citation:0‡Determining Liability in AI-related Medical Errors.pdf](sediment://file_00000000d92c71f5b306b74bae0d4626)

## Overview
AI is increasingly used in clinical contexts including **diagnosis, medical imaging, robotic-assisted surgery, and forecasting models**. This creates new accountability questions when AI contributes to patient harm.
